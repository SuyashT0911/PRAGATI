FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive.

How quickly do you respond to messages?
We aim to respond within 24 hours on weekdays (Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm IST). On weekends and holidays, we may take up to 48 hours. Complex technical issues may take slightly longer.
I found an error in a path or salary data. How do I report it?
Please use the contact form above and select "Content Suggestion" as the topic. Tell us the path name and what information seems incorrect. We review and update data quarterly based on your feedback.
Can I partner with Pragati for college awareness programs?
Absolutely! We love collaborating with schools, colleges, NGOs, and education organizations. Select "Partnership / Collaboration" in the subject dropdown and tell us about your organization and vision.
I'm having trouble logging into my account. Who do I contact?
Use the contact form and select "Technical Issue". Include your registered email address in the message. Our team will reset your access within 24 hours.
Can I suggest a new education path or course to add?
Yes, we love suggestions! Select "Content Suggestion" and describe the path or course you'd like to see. If enough students request the same thing, we prioritize it for our next update cycle.
